About us

WELCOME TO THE CLUB

Welcome to the Ball-It club,where you can change the way you play basketball, improve your skills , master skills you that you're having dificulties with. Ball-It is a basketball team formed to teach youngsters and adults the game of basketball. We train them for the future,train them to become greater than they already are, we train them so they can become legends of the game of basketball. Ball-It club have different generations; Seniors,Juniors,Kadets we even teach the kids, the Mini Basket. We play in different tournaments , inside the country , outside the country , even in the other states. We take generations to camps in summer, having some good times with players and coaches.

"Everybody has talent,but ability takes hard work" Michael Jordan

OUR GOAL

We believe that basketball should become No. 1 sport among youngsters and adults.

QUALIFIED COACHES

Our club has a team of qualified basketball coaches that take care of your success.

GREAT TEAMWORK

Teamwork is one of the main principles that we pursue at our Basketball Club.
